UG Rules (Roblox Game)

These are the core rules for playing Untitled Gears. Violations may result in warnings, kicks, or bans.

  • No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
  • No cheating, exploiting, or using unauthorized scripts.
  • No spamming or flooding chat.
  • Respect other players and staff.
  • No advertising other games or servers without permission.
  • Follow all Roblox Community Standards.

How to Appropriately Address Rule Breakers

As staff, follow this protocol:

  1. Observe and document the violation (screenshots/logs).
  2. Issue a verbal warning in chat or private message.
  3. If repeated, apply a temporary mute or kick.
  4. For severe or repeated offenses, ban the player and report to admins.
  5. Always remain professional and unbiased.

These are the guiding principles for the UG Roblox game—think of them as your moral compass! If someone drifts off course, we'll apply fair and fitting consequences in-game.

What is the Dashboard Used For?

The UGAI Staff Dashboard is a control panel for staff members to manage notifications, access tools, and view system information. It helps in monitoring tasks, enforcing rules in the Roblox game, and maintaining staff accounts.

What is Allowed?

  • Logging in with assigned credentials.
  • Reviewing and acting on notifications.
  • Using staff tools for game moderation and calculations.
  • Viewing system info and instructions.
  • Logging out when finished.

What is Not Allowed?

  • Sharing login credentials with others.
  • Attempting to modify the dashboard code or data.
  • Using the dashboard for non-UGAI related activities.
  • Ignoring notifications or tasks.
  • Abusing moderation tools in the game.

Risks for Account Deletion

Your staff account may be at risk of deletion if:

  • You violate UGAI rules or Roblox standards.
  • You share sensitive information or credentials.
  • You fail to complete assigned tasks repeatedly.
  • There is evidence of unauthorized access or hacking attempts.
  • You engage in misconduct as a staff member.

Always act responsibly to protect your account and maintain your administrative privileges. Failure to do so may result in actions being taken against your privileges, including those on Discord and Roblox.
